Hello World!

I am a doctoral student at the Universität Innsbruck, working in the Department of Mathematics. I am generally interested in category theory and its applications, and I currently work in categorical probability under the guidance of Tobias Fritz. I am also interested in other work with categorical themes, such as probabilistic programming, denotational semantics and type theory.

Academics aside, I enjoy reading and listening to music or podcasts (particularly when I’m engaged in “grunt” work).

I completed a Master degree in Mathematics at the Universität Regensburg under the supervision of Denis-Charles Cisinski. I earned my Bachelors degree from the Chennai Mathematical Institute.
